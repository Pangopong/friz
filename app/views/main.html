IT Safesea

Starting from 2008, IT Systems International has launched the IT Safesea - a new software solution developed by an enthusiastic and professional team.

IT Safesea is a Vessel Traffic Management and Control system and the main area of interest are Ports, Harbors and Coastal Areas. The purpose of any Vessel Traffic Management and Information Systems (VTMS) is to provide the operator with a clear and concise real-time portrayal of vessel movements and interactions in the surveillance area in order to support on-duty decisions.

Electronic Card Solutions

Electronic Card Solution (ECS) is a integrated card management software system designed for online card-based transactions that take place at multiple points of sale in the retail industry (e.g. gas stations, chain stores, etc.).

Different types: RFID, Magnetic stripe, Smart Cards or a combination of all these;

Different functions: Debit, Credit, Pre-paid, Fidelity, Cards for employees, Cards for internal use; the system also accepts 3rd Party Cards;

Different constraints (colors) defined as default: Standard, Silver, Gold. The default color can be modified upon the B2B specific contract clauses.

ITS Locator

The system offers support not only to monitor the location with minimal error margin, but also to trigger automated alerts when several pre-defined events occur. In these situations, the handset application is capable of sending information to emergency numbers either by direct call or via ITS Locator server processes. Based on the gravity of the situation, some procedures may imply recorded voice messages to assist the handset holder or even direct talk to instruct emergency measures.

Features

The VTS system uses multiple components (hardware, software, sensors) and provides basic functionality, interfaces and integration capabilities such as:

Multi sensor processing (Radar, AIS, RDF, CCTV, Meteo)
Advanced radar and data processing
User-friendly Traffic Display System, GIS centric
Integrated recording & replay (data/voice)
Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMIS = VTS + Database)
Traffic simulation tools for training purposes

All IT Safesea VTMS components can be configured to build systems ranging from small to very large ones, due to its modular design and the ability to interface other sensors like AIS, RDF and meteorological sensors.

To increase the safety in the movement area a Conflict Detection unit is an essential part of the IT Safesea system to allow for automatic detection of future potential traffic conflicts or situations.

The Conflict Detection is based on the evaluation and prediction of the movement of tracks obtained by processing the sensor data and topology data (restricted areas, etc.) the traffic situation is analyzed to trigger configured events and traffic rule infringements, such as:

A track crossing an active guard line
A track entering an active guard ring or a guard area
A track leaving a buoy ring or a swing circle
A track exceeding an area specific speed limit
A track entering an active prohibited or restricted area
A track entering a special area slaved to another track (area collision conflict)
Two selected tracks can be on collision course, etc.
Synchronized REPLAY function using GIS, video and voice records

Technologies

Electronic Card Solution is a multi-tier solution that incorporates:

The Card Management Application Server [AppServer] - built on a J2EE platform; implements the Business Logics and the communication gateway for all the other software components in the system;

The Card Management Transaction Server [TransactServer] - provides the mechanism to authenticate and manage the transactions requested from different POSs in the system;

The Card Management Database Server [DBServer] - implements the Business Logics constraints; stores all the information coming from the AppServer;

The Card Management WebServer - implements the browser-based user interfaces and sends them over a SSL connection; the integrated Customer Information Center gives customers access to their related information over the Internet.

The entire solution is built on a J2EE platform and Oracle 10g is used in the back-end, a powerful and secure database engine, which confers a high level of scalability and portability.

How Does It Work?

ITS Locator can be used as an emergency cellular phone application, with an automatic feature to dial the emergency number and with the capability of sending location information for immediate assistance in case of emergency. ITS Locator can also be used for real-time monitoring over the Internet, by sending GPS messages to a control center.

The system offers location service for children, family members, VIPs, valuable property, as well as for mobile sales force, mobile assets or teams deployed in field operations.
